Favorite Book

12 Rules for Life by Jordan B. Peterson

12 Rules for Life An Antidote to Chaos by Dr. Jordan Peterson is a great book to combat the chaos that society presents to us in today's world. It's almost a self help book, only Dr. Peterson allows you to think your way through Chaos instead of giving you a straight solution for everything. 12 Rules is a great book for those who are struggling to find some moment of relief or peace in their lives. If you like psychology, you'll love the book! The only thing I would say is that before reading, you must believe in Dr. Peterson's assumption that God exists, and that the Bible is more than a collection of fairy tales.
